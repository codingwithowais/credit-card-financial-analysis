# Reports

This folder contains the final Power BI dashboard reports exported in PDF format.

## Included Reports
- **Credit Card Transaction Report**  
  Focuses on revenue analysis based on transaction and financial factors such as card category, transaction type, interest earned, and weekly trends.

- **Credit Card Customer Report**  
  Focuses on customer-based revenue analysis including demographics, income groups, customer segmentation, and identification of high-value customers.

These reports represent the final analytical output of the project and are intended for business review and decision-making.
